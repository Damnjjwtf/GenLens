# Score GenLens Signal

Score each candidate item for working Gen AD relevance.

High-signal items include:

- New tool/model releases that change visual production workflows.
- Concrete time/cost deltas.
- Production case studies with process detail.
- Rendering, VFX, AI video, digital human, product photo, design, fashion, audio, or ArchViz pipeline changes.
- Legal/compliance updates affecting synthetic talent or commercial production.
- Infrastructure changes that affect cost, speed, quality, or accessibility.

Low-signal items include:

- Generic brand news.
- Celebrity/personnel news without workflow impact.
- Broad culture posts.
- Tutorials with no new tool or production implication.
- Community chatter without verification.

Never fabricate a delta. If impact is unclear, label as a lead, not a signal.
